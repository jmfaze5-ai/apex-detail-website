What Bonds to the Paint

Chemistry,
Not Just Polish.

Multi-stage machine polishing removes every defect before any ceramic touches the car — coating over flaws locks them in permanently. The 9H coat bonds chemically to the clear coat rather than sitting on top. Once cured (48 hrs), water sheets off, UV can't fade it, and light scratches don't cut through. A hand wash every 8–12 weeks keeps it active. Lasts 2–3 years.
